# Escanova AI Support Agent

Escanova is an AI-powered support ticket escalation system designed to automate customer complaint handling, classification, and escalation workflows. The system intelligently analyzes incoming complaints, assigns priority levels, creates support cases, and automatically notifies the appropriate support team through workflow automation.

The goal of Escanova is to reduce manual intervention in support management while ensuring that high-priority customer issues are escalated quickly and handled efficiently.

---

## Overview

Modern organizations receive a large volume of support requests from customers. Handling these requests manually often leads to delayed responses, missed escalations, and inefficient workflows.

Escanova solves this problem by introducing an automated escalation pipeline that classifies complaints, determines their severity, and routes them through a structured support hierarchy.

The system uses automation workflows to ensure that customer issues are handled based on their urgency and importance.

---

## Key Features

### AI-Based Ticket Classification
Incoming customer complaints are analyzed using keyword-based logic to automatically classify the ticket into priority levels such as **L1, L2, or L3**. This helps ensure that critical issues are identified and handled faster.

### Automated Support Case Creation
Once a complaint is received, Escanova automatically generates a support case with relevant information such as complaint description, subject, and origin.

### Priority-Based Escalation System
Tickets are categorized into escalation levels:

- **L1 – Low Priority Issues**
- **L2 – Medium Priority Issues**
- **L3 – High Priority / Critical Issues**

Each level triggers different automated actions and notifications.

### Email Alert Notifications
Escanova sends automated email alerts to the appropriate stakeholders depending on the escalation level. This ensures that responsible teams are notified immediately when a critical issue arises.

### Chatter Notifications
Internal collaboration is enabled through **Chatter notifications**, allowing support teams to communicate and coordinate directly within the system.

### Escalation Workflow Automation
The system automatically escalates cases when specific conditions are met, ensuring that unresolved issues move up the support chain.

### Dashboard Monitoring
Support teams can monitor support activity through dashboards that provide visibility into:

- Active support cases
- Escalation levels
- Priority distribution
- Case status

### Filtered Case Views
Escanova provides filtered views for support teams to quickly identify cases based on their escalation level:

- **L1 Case View**
- **L2 Case View**
- **L3 Case View**

This allows teams to focus on the issues most relevant to their role.

### Automated Communication Workflow
The system automatically triggers messages and alerts based on case status, ensuring that support staff remain informed about escalations and updates.

---

## Automation Logic

The system operates using two primary automation workflows:

### Complaint Processing Workflow
When a customer complaint is received, the system analyzes the complaint text, determines the escalation level, and creates a new support case.

### Case Escalation Workflow
Once a support case is created, Escanova evaluates the case's escalation level and performs automated actions such as sending email alerts, posting Chatter messages, or escalating the issue to higher management.
